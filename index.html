Info About Breast Cancer!


Cancer is a disease in which cells in the body grow out of control. Except for skin cancer,
breast cancer is the most common cancer in women in the United States.


Our Support.


Research has shown that emotional support from family and friends
can make a big difference to the quality of life of someone with cancer.


Treatment.


If you’ve been diagnosed with breast cancer,
your cancer care team will discuss your treatment options with you. It’s important that you think carefully about each of your choices


Test.


We provide periodic examinations to ensure that each woman lives better
and to help her realize that she is suffering from the disease early.
